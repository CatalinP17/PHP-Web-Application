Web Application (Online Cheese Merchant)
- Password/Login capabilities.
- Search for products on website. Split into categories and fields.
- Add to basket WITHOUT having to Login.
- Capabilities to purchase items in basket.
- Fulfil ticket order (purchase) by submitting name, address etc. (no payment details).
- Easy to update with new products - Dynamic front page.
- Add events (date, time, duration and location) and be able to allow customers to book tickets for said events.
- Show promotions on front page (discounts on products and tickets etc)
